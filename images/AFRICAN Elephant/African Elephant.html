African Elephant

The African elephant is the largest land animal on Earth, known for its massive size and distinctive ears. They are highly intelligent and social animals.

Habitat

African elephants inhabit various ecosystems, including savannas, forests, and deserts across sub-Saharan Africa.

Diet

These herbivores primarily feed on grasses, leaves, fruits, and bark, consuming up to 300 pounds of food each day.

Interesting Fact

African elephants use infrasound to communicate over long distances, which is crucial for their social structure.
