Penguin

Penguins are flightless birds known for their distinctive black and white coloring. They are primarily found in the Southern Hemisphere, especially in Antarctica.

Habitat

Penguins typically inhabit cold regions and are known for their adaptability to life in water, where they spend a significant amount of time hunting for food.

Diet

Penguins primarily eat fish, squid, and krill, relying on their excellent swimming skills to catch prey underwater.

Interesting Fact

Some species of penguins can dive to depths of over 1,800 feet in search of food, making them remarkable divers.
