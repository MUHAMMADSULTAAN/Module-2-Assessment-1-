Lion

The lion is a large cat species known for its majestic mane and social behavior. They are often referred to as the "king of the jungle."

Habitat

Lions primarily inhabit grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands in Africa and a small population in India.

Diet

Lions are carnivorous and mainly hunt large ungulates, such as zebras and wildebeests, as well as smaller animals when needed.

Interesting Fact

Lions are unique among cats for their social structure, living in groups called prides, which consist of related females and their cubs, along with one or more males.
