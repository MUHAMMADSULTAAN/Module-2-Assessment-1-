Flamingo

The flamingo is a wading bird known for its distinctive pink color and long legs. These beautiful birds are often found in large colonies in shallow waters.

Habitat

Flamingos typically inhabit shallow lakes, lagoons, and swamps, where they can find plenty of food.

Diet

They feed primarily on algae, crustaceans, and small invertebrates, which contribute to their pink coloration.

Interesting Fact

Flamingos are social birds and live in large groups, which helps them protect against predators.
