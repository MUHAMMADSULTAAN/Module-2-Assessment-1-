Panda

The giant panda is a bear species native to China, known for its distinctive black and white coloring. Pandas are primarily herbivorous, with bamboo making up most of their diet.

Habitat

Pandas live in mountainous regions of central China, primarily in temperate forests where bamboo is abundant.

Diet

While pandas are technically carnivores, their diet consists of over 99% bamboo. They can consume up to 40 pounds of bamboo daily.

Interesting Fact

Pandas have a special adaptation in their wrist bones that allows them to grip bamboo while eating, making them efficient feeders.
