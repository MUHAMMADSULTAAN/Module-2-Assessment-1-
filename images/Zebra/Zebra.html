Zebra

Zebras are known for their distinctive black and white stripes, which are unique to each individual. They are social animals that live in herds.

Habitat

Zebras are found in various habitats in Africa, including savannas, grasslands, and woodlands.

Diet

Their diet consists mainly of grasses, leaves, and shoots. They are grazing animals and spend a lot of time feeding.

Interesting Fact

Zebras communicate through vocalizations, body language, and facial expressions, and they can recognize each other by their stripes.
